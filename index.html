Well Hello There,

Allow me to introduce myself, my name is Ben Bourgeois. I am from the city of Thibodaux, Louisiana. Its a small corner of the world but we love it down here. Anyway, I started photography around 10 years ago and have loved it ever since when I first did a project on it in high school. I started doing some freelance work and even do some side work with a friend! I started getting into photography around 2012 and I figured why not do my senior project on the subject. So, as any kid would, got my parents to get me my first camera. It was a Nikon D3100 and I'm pretty sure it went out of production but I treated it like royalty. I met up with my friend I work with on the side now and have our own business. It has been around 7 years now since I've started it and I regret nothing. I pride myself on trying to get that perfect shot no matter how long it takes whether its a shot of nature or a photoshoot with a family with a grumpy kid. Along the way I've learned the ins and outs of Photoshop, Illustrator and still learning many programs as I go. I have also upgraded my camera since then to a Canon D77, I'll leave a link to that camera if you would like to check it out yourself. I hope you enjoy your visit into my little world of photography and remember to have a Wonderful Life!
